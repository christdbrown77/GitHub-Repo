ABOUT US

Across the UK, 1 in 6 people have some form of hearing loss and everyday people are making the courageous step in looking for help to hear better. Here at The Hearing Health Clinic, we strive to support these individuals by creating an environment of trust, empathy, understanding and professionalism so that you can make a fully informed decision regarding the treatment of your hearing loss.

Hearing Health Clinic is proudly independent and able to offer our clients the best solutions from the world’s top brands. We never, ever pay commissions or set sales targets on hearing aid fittings or any other product or service. Our priority is to provide you with a professional assessment, unbiased advice, and information in order that our clients can make fully informed decisions.

Services

Ear wax removal

The micro suction technique, performed by our HCPC registered audiologist, uses gentle suction to clear the blocked ear canals, it is much more comfortable and safer in Comparison to other methods such as syringing.

A thin probe is directed at the ear wax and a gentle suction is applied to remove the troublesome ear wax.

The appointment should last no more than 30 minutes approximately.

Hearing Health Checks

During the Hearing Health Check, an inspection of the ear, the ear canal and the ear drum will be performed to ensure everything is healthy. We will perform a screening test which will indicate if you require further investigation into any possible hearing loss.

This appointment is ideal for those who are unsure if they require further investigation and may have some questions, they would like answered by a hearing professional.

Hearing aid consultations

During our Hearing Aid Consultations, our HCPC registered audiologist will perform a full hearing test investigating the health of the ears and any hearing loss that may be present. We will explain the results and nature of any hearing loss that was detected during testing.

The audiologist will discuss the appropriate treatment options that are available, be it a referral to an ENT department if necessary or a discussion regarding the different types of hearing aid technology that is available.

By the end of this appointment you will have been provided with as much information as possible regarding hearing loss and hearing aid technology options so you can make a decision regarding the treatment of your hearing loss that is best for you.

The Hearing Aid Consultation fee is return as discount if you decide to go ahead with hearing aid technology.

Become a Hearing Health Hero

Is your grandmother struggling to follow a conversation, is your father misunderstanding what was said to him, is your husband or wife having the TV up too loud or struggling in groups, why not support them and become their Hearing Health Hero.

A Hearing Health Hero is a person who will support and give encouragement to those struggling with their hearing loss.

Helping them through their rehabilitation journey so that they can become a successful hearing aid user and be part of conversation rather than an observer. Hearing loss can leave people feeling cut off from those they are close too, isolated and withdrawn, increasing the risk of social isolation loneliness and depression, but you can help by being their Hearing Health Hero.

Contact The Hearing Health Clinic giving the information for the person you want to support (with their consent) through their hearing aid journey. We will arrange an appointment so you both can attend the clinic, gaining an understanding of the nature of hearing loss and its impact on an individual.

We recommend you view our hearing aid price lists before your appointment so that you have an idea of how much hearing aid technology can cost.

If the person you are supporting decides to pursue the use of hearing aids and successfully completes their rehabilitation journey you as the Hearing Health Hero will be rewarded with £150*.

Pricing

Hearing Aids – Do you know what you are paying for?

At first glance hearing aid prices can appear to be expensive however once the cost of the hearing aids are divided by the approximate life span that they are expected to last and worn they can be the equivalent to most mobile phone contracts. The main difference being that the payment of hearing aids is made in full at the time of fitting.

It is important to remember that hearing aids are worn continuously throughout the day, 365 days a year, and that the price includes the professional service of the hearing professional to provide an aftercare service for as long as you wish to avail of it. At the Hearing Health Clinic, we are always open, honest, and clear with our information and that applies to our charges for hearing aid technology.

This gives you the opportunity to compare technology options (where possible not all providers disclose their prices), and although you will still need to attend an appointment so the hearing professional can help you figure out what technology is most appropriate to your life style and budget, the ability to compare technology options will ensure you are getting the best value for your investment.
