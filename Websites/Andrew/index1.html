EAR WAX REMOVAL

The micro suction technique uses gentle suction to clear the blocked canals of ear wax, it is much more comfortable and safer in comparison to other methods such as syringing or irrigation.

No ear drops required prior to the appointment, so no need to apply ear drops for days before the appointment.

HEARING TESTS

Across the UK, 1 in 6 people have some form of hearing loss and everyday people are making the courageous step in seeking help to hear better.

Here at the HHC, we strive to support these individuals by creating a place of trust, understanding and professionalism to help make a fully informed decision regarding the treatment of your hearing loss.

HEARING AID TECHNOLOGIES

We are a fully independent hearing clinic with access to a wide range of hearing aid technologies and styles to suit your needs, requirements, and budget.

We offer a trial period with the hearing aid technologies so that you can feel the benefit and be confident in your decision making and with our all-inclusive service package you can feel secure about your future ongoing care.
